About us

The mission and vocation of the fund is to provide comprehensive assistance to the needy.

At the origins of the fund are people of good will. Young, ambitious, who can not stay away from the problems of others, especially when it comes to the older generation. We started our activity in 2020 with a group of volunteers. But in the beginning, realizing that you can't achieve much on your own, we organized a youth charity fund "NEXT".

  • Our organization welcomes investors of all kinds
  • Participate in special events and do good deeds
  • Send donations online and make this world a better place
